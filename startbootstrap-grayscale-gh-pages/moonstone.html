Moonstone's Significance

Moonstone Beach is a very special place that my boyfriend and I visit every chance we get. Since living in Utah, whenever we have the opportunity to go home, even in the winter, we take the chance to go sit on the beach. This beach is gorgeous and even when it is too cold to get into the water, we have an amazing time collecting the colorful pebbles and finding sea glass.

Shoreline

Unlike many beaches, Moonstone's shoreline isn't sandy. Instead the entire beach is covered in colorful rocks, sea glass, and moonstones. There are many neutral colors as well as very bright and vibrant colors. The rocks don't get hot like sand does and walking on the small rocks is a cool sensation.

Sunsets

Sunsets are gorgeous at Moonstone Beach although it usually gets pretty windy and cold as the sun goes down. There's nothing like watching the sun sink down into the ocean at the end of a day at the beach.

Moonstone Park

Right before getting onto the beach, there is a park which has a playset for kids, a lot of grassy area for relaxing and playing yard games. There's also a large public bathroom and a pool that opens up in the summer.
